Gluten-Free Recipe Package

10 simple gluten-free recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Always double-check labels to make sure every ingredient you buy is certified gluten-free.

Save this page or print it as a PDF. Recipes are designed without wheat, barley, or rye ingredients. Check your spice blends, sauces, oats, and broths for hidden gluten before cooking.

1. Garlic Butter Chicken Skillet

Ingredients

  • Chicken cutlets
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Thyme
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper.
  2. Sear chicken in olive oil for 2 to 3 minutes per side until cooked through.
  3. Melt butter with garlic, honey, and parsley.
  4. Return chicken to pan, spoon sauce over the top, and serve hot.

2. Salmon Avocado Plate

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Avocado
  • Mixed greens
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  2. Bake or sear until flaky.
  3. Slice avocado and plate with greens.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.

3. Chickpea Power Salad

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Feta
  • Lemon dressing (gluten-free)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain chickpeas.
  2. Chop vegetables.
  3. Mix chickpeas, vegetables, and feta.
  4. Toss with a gluten-free lemon dressing and chill.

4. Chicken Fajita Cauliflower Rice Bowl

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Cauliflower rice
  • Gluten-free fajita seasoning
  • Lime

Instructions

  1. Slice chicken, peppers, and onion.
  2. Season chicken with gluten-free fajita seasoning.
  3. Sauté chicken, peppers, and onion in olive oil until cooked through.
  4. Serve over warm cauliflower rice with a squeeze of lime.

5. Greek Yogurt Berry Bowl

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Chia seeds
  • Almonds

Instructions

  1. Spoon yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Add berries on top.
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds and almonds.
  4. Serve chilled for breakfast or snack.

6. Turkey Taco Lettuce Cups

Ingredients

  • Lean ground turkey
  • Romaine leaves
  • Gluten-free taco seasoning
  • Tomato
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Brown turkey in a skillet.
  2. Add gluten-free taco seasoning and a splash of water.
  3. Spoon turkey into lettuce leaves.
  4. Top with tomato, avocado, and lime.

7. Cajun Cod with Green Beans

Ingredients

  • Cod fillets
  • Gluten-free Cajun seasoning
  • Green beans
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Pat cod dry and season generously with gluten-free Cajun seasoning.
  2. Sear in olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes per side until flaky.
  3. Steam or sauté green beans with olive oil and salt.
  4. Plate cod with green beans and a wedge of lemon.

8. Apple Almond Protein Plate

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Almond butter
  • Boiled eggs
  • Cottage cheese
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Slice apple.
  2. Add almond butter on the side.
  3. Plate with boiled eggs and cottage cheese.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon and serve.

9. Egg-White Breakfast Skillet

Ingredients

  • Egg whites
  • Spinach
  • Bell peppers
  • Gluten-free turkey sausage
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook gluten-free turkey sausage pieces in a skillet.
  2. Add peppers and spinach.
  3. Pour in egg whites.
  4. Cook until set and top with salsa.

10. Protein Snack Builder

Ingredients

  • Turkey slices (gluten-free)
  • Cheese stick
  • Grapes
  • Carrots
  • Hummus

Instructions

  1. Add turkey slices and cheese stick to a container.
  2. Add grapes and carrots.
  3. Spoon hummus into a small cup.
  4. Keep chilled until ready to eat.
