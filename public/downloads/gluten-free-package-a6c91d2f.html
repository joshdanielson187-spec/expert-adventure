Greek Yogurt Berry Protein Bowl Serves: 1 Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min Meal: Breakfast INGREDIENTS 1 cup plain Greek yogurt, 2% or full-fat 1/2 cup blueberries 1/2 cup sliced strawberries 1 tbsp chia seeds 2 tbsp sliced almonds 1 tsp honey, optional 1/4 tsp cinnamon INSTRUCTIONS Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl. Add blueberries and sliced strawberries. Sprinkle chia seeds, almonds, and cinnamon on top. Drizzle with honey if desired and serve cold. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 360 Protein: about 30g Carbs: about 34g Fat: about 13g Fiber: about 8g MEAL PREP NOTES Keep berries and almonds separate until serving so the bowl stays fresh. Store up to 3 days refrigerated. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Do not add regular granola. Use certified gluten-free granola only. 2. Banana Protein Oats — Certified GF Serves: 1 Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min overnight Meal: Breakfast INGREDIENTS 1/2 cup certified gluten-free rolled oats 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk 1/2 banana, mashed 1/2 scoop gluten-free vanilla protein powder 1 tbsp chia seeds 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter 1/4 tsp cinnamon INSTRUCTIONS Add oats, almond milk, mashed banana, protein powder, chia seeds, and cinnamon to a jar. Stir well until protein powder is fully mixed in. Cover and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, stir and top with peanut butter or almond butter. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 430 Protein: about 28g Carbs: about 52g Fat: about 14g Fiber: about 10g MEAL PREP NOTES Make 3 jars at once for busy mornings. Use within 3 days. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Only use oats labeled certified gluten-free because regular oats may be cross-contaminated. 3. Veggie Egg Scramble Serves: 1 Prep: 8 min Cook: 8 min Meal: Breakfast INGREDIENTS 2 large eggs 2 egg whites 1 cup baby spinach 1/2 cup diced bell pepper 1/4 cup diced onion 1/4 avocado, sliced 1 tsp olive oil 1/4 tsp sea salt 1/8 tsp black pepper INSTRUCTIONS Whisk eggs and egg whites in a bowl. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add bell pepper and onion; cook 3 minutes. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Pour eggs into the skillet and stir gently until cooked through. Top with avocado and serve. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 350 Protein: about 28g Carbs: about 13g Fat: about 22g Fiber: about 5g MEAL PREP NOTES Pre-chop peppers and onions for 3 days of fast breakfasts. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Skip toast unless using certified gluten-free bread. 4. Apple Almond Protein Plate Serves: 1 Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min Meal: Breakfast or snack INGREDIENTS 1 medium apple, sliced 2 tbsp almond butter 2 hard-boiled eggs 1/4 cup cottage cheese 1/4 tsp cinnamon INSTRUCTIONS Slice apple into wedges. Plate apple with almond butter for dipping. Add hard-boiled eggs and cottage cheese. Sprinkle cinnamon over the apple and serve. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 480 Protein: about 31g Carbs: about 33g Fat: about 27g Fiber: about 7g MEAL PREP NOTES Boil eggs ahead and store up to 5 days in the refrigerator. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Use plain cottage cheese and check labels on flavored varieties. 5. Chickpea Power Salad Serves: 2 Prep: 15 min Cook: 0 min Meal: Lunch INGREDIENTS 1 can chickpeas, 15 oz, rinsed and drained 1 cup diced cucumber 1 cup diced red bell pepper 1/4 cup diced red onion 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese 2 tbsp chopped parsley 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp gluten-free Dijon mustard 1/4 tsp sea salt 1/8 tsp black pepper INSTRUCTIONS Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Add chickpeas, cucumber, bell pepper, onion, feta, and parsley to a bowl. Pour dressing over the salad and toss well. Chill 10 minutes before serving. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 430 Protein: about 18g Carbs: about 45g Fat: about 22g Fiber: about 11g MEAL PREP NOTES Stores well up to 4 days. Keep dressing separate for best texture. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Make sure Dijon mustard is gluten-free. 6. Turkey Taco Lettuce Cups Serves: 2 Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Meal: Lunch INGREDIENTS 1 lb lean ground turkey, 93/7 8 large romaine lettuce leaves 2 tbsp gluten-free taco seasoning 1/4 cup water 1/2 cup diced tomato 1/2 avocado, diced 2 tbsp chopped cilantro 1 lime, cut into wedges INSTRUCTIONS Brown ground turkey in a skillet over medium-high heat for 6–7 minutes. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer 2–3 minutes until the turkey is coated and the liquid thickens. Spoon turkey into romaine leaves. Top with tomato, avocado, cilantro, and lime juice. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 455 Protein: about 44g Carbs: about 14g Fat: about 25g Fiber: about 6g MEAL PREP NOTES Cook turkey ahead and store up to 4 days. Keep lettuce separate until serving. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Use gluten-free taco seasoning or make your own with chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, and salt. 7. Mediterranean Chicken Power Bowl Serves: 2 Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min Meal: Lunch INGREDIENTS 2 chicken breasts, about 6 oz each 1 cup cooked quinoa 1 cup diced cucumber 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/4 cup sliced olives 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp dried oregano 1/2 tsp garlic powder 1/4 tsp sea salt 1/8 tsp black pepper INSTRUCTIONS Season chicken with oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook chicken in a skillet over medium heat for 6–7 minutes per side, until it reaches 165°F. Let chicken rest 5 minutes, then slice. Divide quinoa between two bowls. Add cucumber, tomatoes, olives, feta, and sliced chicken. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 540 Protein: about 46g Carbs: about 34g Fat: about 25g Fiber: about 6g MEAL PREP NOTES Cook quinoa and chicken ahead. Store toppings separately for up to 4 days. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Quinoa is naturally gluten-free, but choose a brand labeled gluten-free if sensitive to cross-contamination. 8. Chicken Caesar Lettuce Wraps Serves: 2 Prep: 10 min Cook: 0 min if chicken is cooked Meal: Lunch INGREDIENTS 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or sliced, about 12 oz 8 large romaine lettuce leaves 1/4 cup gluten-free Caesar dressing 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese 1/2 cup diced cucumber 1/4 tsp black pepper Optional: 1/2 avocado, sliced INSTRUCTIONS Add cooked chicken to a bowl. Toss chicken with Caesar dressing and black pepper. Spoon chicken into romaine leaves. Add cucumber, parmesan, and avocado if using. Roll like wraps or serve open-faced. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 410 Protein: about 45g Carbs: about 8g Fat: about 22g Fiber: about 3g MEAL PREP NOTES Use leftover grilled chicken. Keep dressing separate until ready to eat. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Skip croutons and verify Caesar dressing is gluten-free. 9. Lemon Herb Salmon Bowl Serves: 2 Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Meal: Dinner INGREDIENTS 2 salmon fillets, 6 oz each 1 cup cooked brown rice 1 cup diced cucumber 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 1/2 avocado, sliced 1 lemon, juiced 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tsp garlic powder 2 tbsp chopped parsley 1/2 tsp sea salt 1/4 tsp black pepper INSTRUCTIONS Preheat oven to 400°F. Place salmon on a lined baking sheet. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil and season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bake 12–15 minutes until salmon flakes easily. Divide rice, cucumber, tomatoes, and avocado into two bowls. Top each bowl with salmon. Finish with lemon juice, parsley, and remaining olive oil. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 560 Protein: about 42g Carbs: about 38g Fat: about 26g Fiber: about 7g MEAL PREP NOTES Store salmon and rice separately from fresh vegetables. Good for 3 days refrigerated. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Brown rice is naturally gluten-free. Avoid seasoning blends unless labeled gluten-free. 10. Garlic Butter Chicken Skillet Serves: 2 Prep: 8 min Cook: 15 min Meal: Dinner INGREDIENTS 2 chicken breasts, about 6 oz each, sliced into cutlets 2 tbsp butter 1 tbsp olive oil 4 garlic cloves, minced 1 tbsp honey 1 tsp paprika 1/2 tsp dried thyme 1/2 tsp sea salt 1/4 tsp black pepper 2 tbsp chopped parsley 2 cups steamed broccoli or 1/2 lb green beans, optional side INSTRUCTIONS Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken 3–4 minutes per side until it reaches 165°F. Remove chicken and lower heat. Add butter, garlic, and honey to the pan. Return chicken to the skillet and spoon sauce over the top. Serve with broccoli or green beans. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 500 Protein: about 45g Carbs: about 16g Fat: about 28g Fiber: about 4g MEAL PREP NOTES Store chicken and vegetables in containers up to 4 days. Reheat gently so the chicken stays tender. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Use pure spices, not seasoning packets with wheat fillers. 11. Beef & Broccoli Tamari Stir-Fry Serves: 2 Prep: 12 min Cook: 12 min Meal: Dinner INGREDIENTS 12 oz lean sirloin or flank steak, sliced thin 3 cups broccoli florets, about 1 medium head 3 tbsp gluten-free tamari 1 tbsp rice vinegar 1 tbsp honey 1 tsp cornstarch labeled gluten-free or arrowroot 2 garlic cloves, minced 1 tsp fresh grated ginger 1 tbsp avocado oil or olive oil 1 cup cooked rice or cauliflower rice INSTRUCTIONS Whisk tamari, rice vinegar, honey, cornstarch, garlic, and ginger in a bowl. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. Sear steak strips for 2–3 minutes, then remove. Add broccoli and 2 tbsp water; cook 4 minutes. Return steak to skillet. Pour sauce over everything and stir until thickened. Serve over rice or cauliflower rice. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 535 Protein: about 42g Carbs: about 44g Fat: about 21g Fiber: about 6g MEAL PREP NOTES Slice steak before cooking for faster weeknight prep. Store up to 3 days. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Use tamari instead of soy sauce. Regular soy sauce usually contains wheat. 12. Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Peppers Serves: 2 Prep: 12 min Cook: 25 min Meal: Dinner INGREDIENTS 4 bell peppers, halved and seeded 2 cups cooked shredded chicken breast, about 12 oz 1/3 cup gluten-free buffalo sauce 1/2 cup cooked rice or cauliflower rice 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt 2 tbsp sliced green onions INSTRUCTIONS Preheat oven to 400°F. Place pepper halves in a baking dish. Mix chicken, buffalo sauce, rice, Greek yogurt, and half the cheese. Stuff mixture into peppers. Top with remaining cheese. Bake 22–25 minutes until peppers soften and filling is hot. Top with green onions and serve. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 505 Protein: about 45g Carbs: about 28g Fat: about 24g Fiber: about 6g MEAL PREP NOTES Assemble peppers ahead and bake when ready. Refrigerate up to 3 days before baking. GLUTEN-FREE SWAP Check buffalo sauce and shredded cheese labels for gluten-containing additives. 13. Protein Energy Bites Serves: 8 bites Prep: 10 min Cook: 0 min Meal: Snack INGREDIENTS 1 cup certified gluten-free rolled oats 1/2 cup natural peanut butter or almond butter 1/3 cup honey 1/2 scoop gluten-free vanilla protein powder 2 tbsp chia seeds 2 tbsp mini dark chocolate chips, gluten-free 1/4 tsp cinnamon INSTRUCTIONS Add all ingredients to a mixing bowl. Stir until a thick dough forms. Roll into 8 equal bites. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before eating. NUTRITION PER BITE Calories: about 155 Protein: about 7g Carbs: about 18g Fat: about 7g Fiber: about 3g MEAL PREP NOTES Store in the fridge for up to 7 days or freeze for up to 2 months. Add pineapple or berries. Top with nuts, cinnamon, and honey if desired. Serve cold. NUTRITION PER SERVING Calories: about 310 Protein: about 30g Carbs: about 28g Fat: about 10g Fiber: about 3g MEAL PREP NOTES Store in sealed containers up to 3 days.
