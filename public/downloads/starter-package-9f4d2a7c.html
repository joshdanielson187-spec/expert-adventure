14 healthy recipe ideas with simple ingredients and step-by-step cooking instructions.

Save this page or print it as a PDF. These recipes are for general meal planning and are not medical advice.

1. Garlic Butter Chicken Skillet

Ingredients

  • Chicken cutlets
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Thyme
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper.
  2. Sear chicken in olive oil for 2 to 3 minutes per side until cooked through.
  3. Melt butter with garlic, honey, Italian seasoning, and parsley.
  4. Return chicken to pan, spoon sauce over the top, and serve hot.

2. Lemon Herb Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Brown rice
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemon
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill or sear chicken until 165°F inside.
  4. Build bowls with rice, vegetables, chicken, and fresh herbs.

3. Turkey Taco Lettuce Cups

Ingredients

  • Lean ground turkey
  • Romaine leaves
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tomato
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Brown turkey in a skillet.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water.
  3. Spoon turkey into lettuce leaves.
  4. Top with tomato, avocado, and lime.

5. Salmon Avocado Plate

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Avocado
  • Mixed greens
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  2. Bake or sear until flaky.
  3. Slice avocado and plate with greens.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.

13. Lemon Herb Salmon Bowl

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Brown rice or quinoa
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Mixed greens
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Season salmon with lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and parsley.
  2. Bake at 400°F for 10 to 14 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily.
  3. Add cooked rice or quinoa to a bowl.
  4. Top with mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, and salmon.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and extra lemon juice before serving.

Nutrition

  • Calories: about 520
  • Protein: about 38g
  • Carbs: about 42g
  • Fat: about 22g

14. Mediterranean Chicken Power Bowl

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Brown rice
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Lemon dressing

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken until it reaches 165°F.
  2. Slice chicken into strips.
  3. Add rice, cucumber, tomatoes, and feta to a bowl.
  4. Top with chicken and lemon dressing.

Nutrition

  • Calories: about 520
  • Protein: about 42g
  • Carbs: about 48g
  • Fat: about 17g
