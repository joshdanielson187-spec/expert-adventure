1. Garlic Butter Chicken Skillet

  • 490 cal
  • 44g protein
  • 15 min
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (6–7 oz each), pounded thin
  • 3 tbsp butter, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika, ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • ½ tsp sea salt, ¼ tsp black pepper, 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Pat chicken dry. Season with paprika, thyme, salt, pepper.
  2. Sear in 1 tbsp hot oil over medium-high, 4–5 min per side until cooked through.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add 3 tbsp butter and garlic; cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Stir in honey. Return chicken, spoon sauce over top. Garnish with parsley.
Protein: 44g
Carbs: 6g
Fat: 22g
Fiber: 0g

Meal Prep Notes

Cook and store chicken for 4 days. Reheat gently. Sauce best made fresh. Serve over cauliflower rice or roasted potatoes.

2. Lemon Herb Chicken Bowl

3. Turkey Taco Lettuce Cups

  • 380 cal
  • 36g protein
  • 20 min
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground turkey (93/7)
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 large head romaine lettuce (8–10 leaves)
  • 1 cup diced tomato, 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro (optional), 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Brown turkey in 1 tbsp oil over medium-high, 6–7 min, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Sprinkle taco seasoning, add water. Simmer 2–3 min until thickened.
  3. Spoon into lettuce cups. Top with tomato, avocado, cilantro. Squeeze lime over.

Carbs: 12g
Fat: 18g
Fiber: 4g

Meal Prep Notes

Cook turkey and store 4 days. Assemble fresh to prevent lettuce from wilting. Avocado best added just before eating.

5. Salmon Avocado Plate

  • 560 cal
  • 40g protein
  • 22 min
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (5–6 oz each)
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced (½ per serving)
  • 4 cups mixed greens (2 cups per serving)
  • 1 lemon (½ for juice, ½ for wedges)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp sea salt, ¼ tsp black pepper, ½ tsp smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Pat salmon dry. Season with salt, pepper, paprika.
  2. Sear skin-side down in 1 tbsp oil, 4 min until crispy. Flip, cook 2–3 min more until flaky.
  3. Toss greens with 1 tbsp oil and lemon juice. Plate greens, fan avocado, top with salmon, lemon wedge.

Carbs: 12g

Fiber: 4g

Meal Prep Notes

Salmon best eaten fresh. Roast broccoli or asparagus as greens substitute. Store components separately.

7. Egg-White Breakfast Skillet

  • 290 cal
  • 32g protein
  • 15 min
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg whites (or 8–10 egg whites)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced (¾ cup)
  • ¼ cup diced onion
  • ½ cup mushrooms, diced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • ½ tsp sea salt, ¼ tsp black pepper, ¼ tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg whites with salt, pepper, garlic powder.
  2. Heat ½ tbsp butter in large skillet over medium-high. Sauté peppers and onion 2–3 min.
  3. Add spinach and mushrooms; cook 1 min until wilted.
  4. Pour egg whites over vegetables. Gently scramble 2–3 min until cooked through but still soft. Top with feta.
Protein: 32g
Carbs: 8g
Fat: 6g
Fiber: 2g

Meal Prep Notes

Dice vegetables on Sunday. Best eaten fresh. Can reheat gently (20–30 sec microwave).

10. Simple Meal-Prep Chicken Box

  • 480 cal
  • 40g protein
  • 0 min (prep day)
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (6–7 oz each), grilled or roasted
  • 1½ cups steamed broccoli florets (12 spears)
  • 1 medium sweet potato, roasted and cubed
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp sea salt, ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder, ½ tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. Grill or bake chicken until cooked through (165°F). Cool slightly.
  2. Steam broccoli until tender-crisp (5–7 min).
  3. Roast sweet potato cubes at 400°F for 20–25 min until soft.
  4. Divide into two meal-prep containers. Drizzle oil over each. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika.
Protein: 40g
Carbs: 28g
Fat: 14g
Fiber: 4g

Meal Prep Notes

Classic Sunday meal-prep lunch. Stores 5 days. Reheat in microwave (2–3 min) or on stovetop. Use as lunch all week.

14. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

  • 520 cal
  • 40g protein
  • 25 min
  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 12 oz flank or sirloin steak, sliced against the grain
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into florets (4 cups)
  • 3 tbsp tamari or low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp cornstarch or arrowroot
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

30. Steak Taco Salad

  • 40g protein

  • Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 12 oz lean steak (sirloin or flank), sliced thin
  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup diced tomato
  • 1 avocado, sliced (½ per serving)
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup black olives, pitted and halved
  • ¼ cup shredded sharp cheddar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
