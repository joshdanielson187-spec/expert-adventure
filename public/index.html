15 min 4 servings

Garlic Butter Chicken Skillet

Tender chicken cutlets seasoned with paprika and thyme, finished with a quick garlic butter honey pan sauce.

Ingredients
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into 4 cutlets
  • 1 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp each garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter, 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning and 1/2 tsp dried parsley
Instructions
  1. Slice chicken lengthwise into four thin cutlets.
  2. Mix salt, paprika, thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper. Season both sides.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high. Sear chicken 2 to 3 minutes per side until 165°F.
  4. Melt butter and stir in garlic, honey, Italian seasoning, and parsley.
  5. Reduce heat, pour sauce into the skillet, scrape browned bits, return chicken, spoon sauce over top, and serve.

Lemon Herb Chicken Bowl

A high-protein bowl with quinoa, broccoli, avocado, and lemon chicken.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb chicken breast, 2 cups cooked quinoa, 3 cups broccoli
  • 1 avocado, 1 lemon, 1 tbsp olive oil, parsley
  • Garlic powder, oregano, salt, pepper
Instructions
  1. Season chicken with lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sear chicken until cooked through, then rest and slice.
  3. Char broccoli in the same pan with a splash of oil.
  4. Build bowls with quinoa, broccoli, chicken, avocado, parsley, and lemon.
20 minlow carb

Turkey Taco Lettuce Cups

Lean taco filling served in crisp lettuce with salsa and Greek yogurt.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey, romaine leaves, 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Tomatoes, cilantro, lime, 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
Instructions
  1. Brown turkey in a skillet and drain excess liquid.
  2. Add spices and a splash of water, then simmer until coated.
  3. Spoon into lettuce leaves and top with salsa, tomatoes, yogurt, cilantro, and lime.

Greek Yogurt Berry Bowl

A fast breakfast with creamy yogurt, berries, chia, granola, and honey.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups nonfat Greek yogurt, 1 cup mixed berries
  • 2 tbsp low-sugar granola, 1 tsp chia seeds, 1 tsp honey
Instructions
  1. Add yogurt to a bowl.
  2. Top with berries, granola, chia, and honey.
  3. Serve cold or pack in a jar for meal prep.

Salmon Avocado Plate

Seared salmon with avocado, cucumber, asparagus, and lemon dill seasoning.

Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets, 1 avocado, asparagus, cucumber
  • Lemon, dill, garlic powder, salt, pepper, olive oil
Instructions

Chickpea Power Salad

A meal-prep salad with chickpeas, greens, feta, tomatoes, and lemon tahini dressing.

Ingredients
  • 1 can chickpeas, 4 cups chopped kale, tomatoes, cucumber
  • 2 tbsp feta, 1 tbsp tahini, lemon juice, garlic, water
Instructions
  1. Massage kale with lemon juice and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, and feta.
  3. Whisk tahini, lemon, garlic, and water. Toss and serve.

Egg-White Breakfast Skillet

A big-volume breakfast with egg whites, peppers, mushrooms, spinach, and feta.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups egg whites, bell pepper, mushrooms, spinach
  • 2 tbsp feta, cooking spray, salt, pepper, hot sauce
Instructions
  1. Sauté peppers and mushrooms until softened.
  2. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. Pour in egg whites and stir gently until set. Finish with feta and hot sauce.

Herb-Crusted Salmon & Sweet Potato

Oven-baked salmon with a Dijon-herb crust served over roasted sweet potato rounds and wilted spinach.

Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets, 2 medium sweet potatoes, 2 cups spinach
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard (GF-certified), fresh dill, parsley
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder
Instructions
  1. Slice sweet potatoes into rounds, toss with olive oil and salt, roast at 425°F for 20 min.
  2. Brush salmon with Dijon, press on chopped herbs and garlic powder.
  3. Bake salmon alongside potatoes for the last 12 min.
  4. Wilt spinach in a hot pan and plate with salmon and potatoes.
20 minGF

Grilled Chicken with Mango Salsa

Juicy grilled chicken breast topped with a bright mango-jalapeño salsa and served over cilantro-lime rice.

Ingredients
  • 2 chicken breasts, 1 ripe mango, 1/2 jalapeño, red onion
  • Cilantro, 2 limes, 1 cup white rice, salt, cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
Instructions
  1. Season chicken with cumin, salt, and lime juice. Grill 5–6 min per side.
  2. Dice mango, jalapeño, onion, and cilantro; toss with lime juice and salt.
  3. Cook rice with a squeeze of lime and chopped cilantro stirred in.
  4. Slice chicken and serve over rice, topped with mango salsa.

Turkey & Zucchini Meatballs

Tender baked meatballs made with ground turkey and shredded zucchini — no breadcrumbs needed.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey, 1 medium zucchini (shredded & squeezed dry)
  • 1 egg, 2 tbsp almond flour, garlic, Italian seasoning
  • Salt, pepper, marinara sauce (GF-certified)
Instructions
  1. Mix turkey, zucchini, egg, almond flour, garlic, and seasonings.
  2. Roll into 16 meatballs and place on a lined baking sheet.
  3. Bake at 400°F for 18–20 min until cooked through.
  4. Warm marinara and serve meatballs over it or alongside roasted veg.
15 minGF

Shrimp Cauliflower Fried Rice

A fast, low-carb stir-fry with shrimp, riced cauliflower, eggs, and tamari instead of soy sauce.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb shrimp (peeled), 4 cups riced cauliflower, 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed peas & carrots, 3 green onions
  • 2 tbsp tamari (GF soy sauce), 1 tbsp sesame oil, garlic
Instructions
  1. Sauté shrimp in sesame oil until pink, then set aside.
  2. Scramble eggs in the same pan, then add cauliflower rice and veggies.
  3. Stir-fry 4–5 min, add tamari and garlic, return shrimp.
  4. Top with sliced green onions and serve.
20 minGF

Lemon Garlic Chicken & Green Beans

One-pan chicken thighs with crispy skin, bright lemon, and blistered green beans.

Ingredients
  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs, 3 cups green beans (trimmed)
  • 4 garlic cloves, 1 lemon, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, dried oregano, red pepper flakes
Instructions
  1. Season thighs with salt, pepper, and oregano. Sear skin-side down 6 min.
  2. Flip, add green beans, garlic, and lemon slices around the chicken.
  3. Transfer to oven at 400°F for 12 min until chicken reaches 165°F.
  4. Squeeze remaining lemon over top and serve.
15 minGF

Beef & Bell Pepper Skillet

Quick-seared beef strips with colorful peppers in a savory tamari-ginger sauce over rice.

Ingredients
  • 1 lb flank steak (sliced thin), 3 bell peppers (mixed colors)
  • 2 tbsp tamari, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic, cooked rice
Instructions
  1. Sear steak strips in hot oil 2 min per side, then set aside.
  2. Stir-fry sliced peppers 3–4 min until tender-crisp.
  3. Return beef, add tamari, vinegar, ginger, and garlic. Toss 1 min.
  4. Serve over rice.
25 minGF

Baked Cod with Tomato & Olive Relish

Flaky white cod topped with a Mediterranean relish of cherry tomatoes, olives, capers, and fresh basil.

Ingredients
  • 2 cod fillets, 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved), 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1 tbsp capers, fresh basil, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon, salt, pepper, garlic
Instructions
  1. Season cod with salt, pepper, and garlic. Place in a baking dish.
  2. Mix tomatoes, olives, capers, basil, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  3. Spoon relish over cod and bake at 400°F for 15–18 min.
  4. Serve with a side of roasted potatoes or steamed greens.
